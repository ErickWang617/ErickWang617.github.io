Simple meals that anyone can make

As a college student, I am often occupied with clubs, sports, and academics. It is difficult to find time in the day to cook. This is a common problem that occurs with students my age. Therefore, this website will be dedicated to showing students some quick and simple meals that are also budget friendly. All meals are around $3-8 per serving, so it will be affordable. Additionally, no dish takes more than half an hour to prepare, leaving plently of time remaining in your day to do whatever is needed.
