Fusilli with Spicy Meat Sauce

Fusili are a variety of pasta that has a very unique, helical shape. In my opinion, they are the best type of pasta since the twirls act as small crevices that can capture a lot of sauce and great amounts of flavor! Fusilli is thicker than the typical pasta styles, so it will take 10-15 minutes for al dente. This recipe does not have a lot of ingredients and is a greate example of a beginner dish. With good multi-tasking and awareness, this dish should not take more than 25 minutes.

Needed Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Fusilli
  • Red Peppers
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Pasta Sauce

Many ingredients are interchangable. For instance, you can use any type of pasta of your choosing. The ground beef used in this photo is 93% lean and 7% fat.

Fusilli with Ground Beef in Spicy Pasta Sauce

Steps:

  1. First boil about 4 quarts of water in a pot and add salt if desired.
  2. Gently drop in the fusilli and take bring the heat down to a low-medium.
  3. Let fusilli cook for 10-15 minutes and stir occasionally
  4. While the pasta is cooking, wash and dice up the red peppers and begin to prepare the meat.
  5. In a separate pan, pour 1-2 teaspoons of oil and throw in your ground beef.
  6. Season the ground beef with salt, black pepper, onion pepper, and garlic pepper while you chop up the ground beef into small chunks in the pan.
  7. Once pasta is finished cooking, drain the water from the pot and add the pasta into the pan with the rest of the ground beef.
  8. Pour in the pasta sauce so it covers most of the pasta and meat.
  9. Add your desired amount of diced red peppers and red pepper flakes.
  10. Cook for about 5 minutes with all the ingredients in the pot.
  11. Plate and enjoy your meal!
